Privacy Policy

Last updated: September 2025

Trade Haven (“we”, “our”, “us”) respects your privacy. This policy explains what we collect, how we use it, and your choices.

Information We Collect

  • Account basics: Pi Network user identifier provided by the Pi SDK for authentication.
  • Transactional data: payment intents, amounts, timestamps and status for purchases you initiate.
  • Technical data: device/browser info, IP-derived region and logs needed to keep the service secure and reliable.

How We Use Information

  • To operate and improve Trade Haven features.
  • To process Pi payments and prevent fraud/abuse.
  • To comply with legal obligations.

Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We may share with infrastructure providers (e.g., hosting, logging, analytics) strictly to run the service, under contractual confidentiality.

Data Retention

We keep data only as long as necessary for the purposes above, then delete or anonymize it.

Your Choices

  • You can disconnect app access via your Pi settings at any time.
  • You may request access or deletion by contacting us (see “Contact”).

Security

We use industry-standard security measures. No method is 100% secure, but we continually improve our protections.

Children

Trade Haven is not intended for children under the age of 13 (or as defined by local law).

International Use

Your data may be processed in countries different from yours, subject to appropriate safeguards.

Changes

We may update this policy. We’ll revise the “Last updated” date and, when appropriate, provide notice in-app.
